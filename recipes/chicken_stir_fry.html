Chicken Stir Fry

Description

Experience the vibrant flavors of a quick and delicious chicken stir-fry. This recipe combines tender chicken, colorful vegetables, and a flavorful sauce for a satisfying and healthy meal. Perfect for busy weeknights when you want a tasty dinner on the table in no time.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup (150g) snap peas
  • 1 cup (150g) broccoli florets
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the sliced chicken, soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch. Mix well and set aside to marinate for about 10 minutes.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the hot oil and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the marinated chicken to the skillet and stir-fry until cooked through and lightly browned. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add the sliced bell peppers, snap peas, and broccoli florets. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  6. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet with the vegetables and stir to combine.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning and sauce consistency as desired.
  8. Remove from heat and serve the chicken stir-fry over steamed rice or noodles.
  9. Enjoy the flavorful and nutritious chicken stir-fry!
